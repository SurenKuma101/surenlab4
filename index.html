Welcome to Pokhara

Explore the picturesque city of Nepal

Introduction

Pokhara is a picturesque city in Nepal, known for its stunning natural beauty, serene lakes, and breathtaking mountain views. It lies about 200 km west of Kathmandu and serves as a gateway to the Annapurna region for trekkers.

Historical Highlights

  • Originally a trade route between Tibet and India, Pokhara has long been a cultural melting pot.
  • It developed significantly after the 1970s with tourism and infrastructure growth.
  • The Gurkha soldiers from this region have gained international recognition for their bravery.

Tourist Attractions

  • Phewa Lake: A beautiful lake with boat rides and views of the Annapurna range.
  • Sarangkot: A popular viewpoint for sunrise and paragliding.
  • Davis Falls & Gupteshwor Cave: A fascinating waterfall and cave with religious significance.
  • World Peace Pagoda: A stupa offering panoramic views of the city and mountains.
